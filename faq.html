20

TRX0 frequency spectrum detected but TRX1 does not seem to be transmitting on the specified frequency. what is could be the issue?

According to the GSM Standard, only TRX0 has to transmit constantly. Secondary TRX's can transmit only when TRX0 would completely loaded. So if you want to see transmission on the TRX1 you should either:
1. have more than 6 active call legs (more than 3 acive calls)
2. set TRX0 configuration to SDCCH and leave TCH only on the TRX1 and make a call.
In other case you'll see that the transmission of TRX1 is not continuous, unless you have all TCH's on TRX1.

How to control/change the process of automatically assigning a number? Where is the source code?

The code which generates the random number is here But we strongly recommend you against modifying the code. We regularly roll-out updated code and you'll be losing your changes. The recommended way of assigning numbers is by out of band means:
1. You can create a simple web-portal for users to register at and claim their numbers.
